Business Impact
Single source of truth linking sales, advance, petty cash, bills, in-house charges, and final P&L to each event.
Full accountability — who changed what, from which login, device, and IP — reducing disputes and uncontrolled access.
Event profitability visible before, during, and after execution — no waiting for manual reconciliation.
Receipt and bill data is event-linked, eliminating missing bills and delayed settlement.
Admin team monitors active sessions and access directly from the UI without IT intervention.
