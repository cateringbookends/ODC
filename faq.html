FAQ

When is KYC required?

KYC details are required only when at least one payment cycle is marked Online.

Can advance be less or more than 50%?

Yes. The advance starts at 50% by default, but the amount is editable.

Can one event have both Cash and Online payments?

Yes. Each billing cycle has its own billing mode, so payments can be split across Cash and Online.
